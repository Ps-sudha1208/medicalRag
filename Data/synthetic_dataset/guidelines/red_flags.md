Post-op red flags requiring urgent clinical review:
- Sepsis concern: fever + tachycardia + rising WBC or hypotension.
- SBP < 90 or new confusion.
- SpO2 < 90 or sudden deterioration.
- Suspected PE: acute hypoxia + tachycardia +/- pleuritic pain.
- Hemorrhage concern: hypotension + tachycardia + falling hemoglobin.
